**Full transparency:** I've used Xero for 2.5 years across two different businesses. Here's the unfiltered version.

### Setup Mistake I Made

I rushed the initial chart of accounts setup. Spent my first month categorizing everything as "general expenses" because I didn't understand Xero's category structure. Month 2, I had to recategorize 200+ transactions manually.

**Lesson:** Spend 30 minutes with Xero's chart of accounts guide BEFORE importing your first transactions. You'll save hours.

### Thing I Wish I Knew Earlier

The mobile app is where Xero shines. For the first 6 months, I only used desktop. Once I started snapping receipt photos the moment I got them, my "missing receipt hunt" dropped to zero.

**Lesson:** Install the mobile app on day one. Use it religiously. Your future self will thank you.

### Actual Time Investment

- **Week 1:** 5-6 hours setting up (accounts, bank feeds, templates)
- **Week 2-3:** 2-3 hours/week learning and adjusting
- **Month 2+:** 30-45 minutes/week maintaining

It's not "set and forget" at first. But after a month, it becomes routine.

### Best Feature Most People Miss

**Bank rules.** Xero can auto-categorize recurring transactions. Starbucks? Always "Meals & Entertainment." Shopify fees? Always "Merchant Fees." Set up 10-15 rules early, and 70% of your reconciliation becomes automatic.

### Biggest Frustration

The reporting interface is powerful but cluttered. It took me 3 months to find the exact report format I needed. QuickBooks' reporting is more intuitive for non-accountants.

**Lesson:** If reporting is critical, watch Xero's reporting tutorial videos before committing.

- Watching Xero's tutorial videos (they're good)

### What if I need help after signing up?

**Xero offers:**
- Email support (24-48 hour response)
- Phone support (business hours, region dependent)
- Xero Central (knowledge base - excellent)
- Community forums
- YouTube tutorials

**What Xero doesn't offer:**
- Live chat (most regions)
- Setup assistance (you're on your own or hire a partner)

### Will my accountant work with Xero?

**Most modern accountants do.** Ask yours directly: "Do you support Xero clients?"

If they say no:
- You can grant them read-only access anyway

- Consider switching accountants (many now require cloud accounting)

**Expect cleanup work** after any migration. Budget 3-5 hours for a year's worth of data.

No system is unhackable, but Xero takes security seriously.

### What happens after the 6-month promotional period?
